Research




I am currently pursuing several projects in the topics of gravitational wave astrophysics and the origin and multimessenger emission of massive black holes.

My research proposal AstroWaveforms, which aims to include astrophysical environmental effects in gravitational wave templates, was recently awarded a Marie Curie fellowship.


If you have any questions or want to get in touch, do not hesitate to contact me at lorenz.zwick96@gmail.com!
